Product Description


Wire Mesh cable-trays can be seen as a very smiplified version of the industrical & commercial cable-trays in use today. This simple grid-like structure, is sturdy, robust & yet so flexible that it can take any desired shape or size.
Our Wire Mesh cable-trays come in stainless steel(SS), & galvanised iron (GI) in various forms & sizes. These materials, especially the SS, are rust-resistant,& very much suitable for industries such as food , pharma,etc.
The simple open design of the cable-tarys provides no space for dust or rodents accumulation and also provides much higher cooling area.
